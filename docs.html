Features of Lossless Scaling

Lossless Scaling offers a suite of advanced features to enhance the visual quality of your games:

  • Fast and Efficient Scaling: Ultra-fast upscaling for smooth gaming experience.
  • Multiple Scaling Options: Choose from a variety of algorithms like AMD FSR, NVIDIA Image Scaling, Integer Scaling, and more.
  • Machine Learning Enhancements: ML-based proprietary algorithms for improved visual fidelity.
